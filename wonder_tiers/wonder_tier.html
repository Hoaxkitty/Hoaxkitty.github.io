Tier 1: God Tier

Bonuses are consistent and game changingly strong.

Alhambra, Colossus ,Great Library, Hanging Gardens, Hubble Space Telescope, Machu Picchu, Notre Dame, Prora, Statue of Liberty, Temple of Artemis

Tier 2: Strong

Bonuses are consistent and strong or situational and game changingly strong.

Borobudur, Brandenburg Gate, Chichen Itza, Eiffel Tower, Forbidden Palace, Great Lighthouse, Great Wall, Leaning Tower of Pisa, Mausoleum of Halicanasus, Petra, Stonehenge, Taj Mahal

Tier 3: Decent

Bonuses are consistent and mediocre or situational and strong.

Hagia Sophia, Kremlin, Neuschwanstein, Oracle, Porcelain Tower, Pyramids, Red Fort, Sistine Chapel

Tier 4: Weak

Bonuses are consistent but weak or situational and mediocre.

Big Ben, Himeji Castle, Parthenon, Terracotta Army

Tier 5: Bad

Bonuses are consistently inconsequential or situational and weak.

Angkor Wat, Broadway, Globe Theatre, Great Mosque of Djenne, Louvre, Statue of Zeus, Sydney Opera House, Uffizi

Tier 6: Awful

Bonuses are detrimental to the civilization.

CN Tower, Cristo Redentor, Great Firewall, Pentagon
