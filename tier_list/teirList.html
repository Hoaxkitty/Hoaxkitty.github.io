Tier 1: God Tier

Bonuses are consistent and game changingly strong

Babylon:

Early and strong science bonuses from free scientist.

Egypt:

Increased Wonder production is invaluable early and late-game.

England:

Longbows have 3 range so they are unbelievably strong and allow for quite easy wars. Ships of the Line are by far the strongest mid-game naval unit and with other bonuses are unstoppable. Extra spys never hurt either.

Ethopia:

Increased defence on cities and Steles give culture and faith which give an amazing head start on all cities and you empire.

Inca:

Decreased road cost in hills and increadable growth from Terrace Farms let you thrive in very defensive positions

Korea:

Consistent science bonuses and 2 powerful defensive units allow for amazing snowballing.

Persia:

Extended golden ages and the bonuses given during them are incredibly strong. Immortals are very strong early againsts barbarians.

Poland:

Free policies can end up giving you an extra tree by late game allowing for more risks to be taken and more flexible game play.

Huns:

Battering Rams are the strongest very early-game units that can give you a whole city head start. Double horses with Horse Archers give great offensive ability on flat land.

Maya:

Free great people. Pyramid gives a great buff to science as well as faith generation.

Tier 2: Strong

Bonuses are consistent and strong or situational and game changingly strong

Arabia:

Camal Archers are superb at warfare and stats wise. Bazaar allows for more trade and gold. Bonus oil is invaluable late-game.

Aztec:

Amazing growth from Floating Gardens, but needs to be near lakes. Jaguar Warrior is great for scouting early game.

China:

Strong early science plus gold building is strong. Chu Ko Nus and great general bonuses are amazing for wars.

Greece:

Under the right cponditions you can never lose influence with City-States. Strong early-game units are great for early conquest and defence.

Russia:

Production bonuses scale well into the late-game and double resources is invaluable for massive wars. Cossaks are great for pushing advantages in wars.

Shoshone:

Extra city tiles means you save alot of gold and culture per city. Scout replacement is very good to get head starts from ruins.

Spain:

Under the right conditions games can be won out of the first few turns if the right wonders are discovered by you first.

Zulu:

Reduced land maintenance is strong. Impis plus Ikanda buffs provide many high level troops that can tear through anything they meet.

Tier 3: Decent

Bonuses are consistent and mediocre or situational and strong

Byzantium:

An extra religion belief is always strong and lets you get an nice early religion. Dromon is the strongest early-game naval unit and is the only ranged one in Classical and Ancient era.

Celts:

Increased faith is nice but your city must be close to many forest tiles to make use of it. Ceilidh Hall is strong and Pictish Warriors provide nice bursts of faith early-game.

Germany:

The chance of incorporating barbarians is nice early game with reduced land maintenance. Hanse gives strong bunuses to gold and production near many City-States.
